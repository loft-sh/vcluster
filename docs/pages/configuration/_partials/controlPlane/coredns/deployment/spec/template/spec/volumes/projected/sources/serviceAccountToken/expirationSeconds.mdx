############ `expirationSeconds` <span className="config-field-required" data-required="false">required</span> <span className="config-field-type">integer</span> <span className="config-field-default"></span> <span className="config-field-enum"></span> {#controlPlane-coredns-deployment-spec-template-spec-volumes-projected-sources-serviceAccountToken-expirationSeconds}

expirationSeconds is the requested duration of validity of the service
account token. As the token approaches expiration, the kubelet volume
plugin will proactively rotate the service account token. The kubelet will
start trying to rotate the token if the token is older than 80 percent of
its time to live or if the token is older than 24 hours.Defaults to 1 hour
and must be at least 10 minutes.
